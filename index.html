About Us

Discover our story in providing services.

Radiant Tech Solutions has built a name and trust with its clients by advising and giving solutions on some of the most important organizational issues, such as advanced analytics, corporate finance, sustainability, organization, marketing, digital content development, information technology, strategy and operations. Incepted on 14th March 2016, headquartered in India, Radiant Tech Solutions is among the top-notch promising companies of the modern times, servicing across diverse regimes, but largely focusing on HR Learning & Development and IT/Technology Consulting /Services/Solutions Domain. At Radiant Tech Solutions, we believe that the ability of an organization to disseminate the power of learning and development can provide a real edge in competition. Likewise, Radiant Tech Solutions believes in providing top notch solutions to companies that believe in making the entire process of Management Consulting – Organisation, HR Development, IT & Digital Transformation smooth and effective for employees and employer. Our Learning & Development and IT development solutions are specifically designed to fit in our client’s requirements.
